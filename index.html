GIFTING

Gifting was a huge challenge. The goal was to make it easier for users to understand and be able to breeze through the checkout gifting options. Another challenge was making the gifting a more personal experience as the recipient is not getting a physical product most of the time, so it needed to emulate “gift opening”. I think that we achieved that with these designs.







LEARNINGS & THOUGHTS

2013 was a big year for me. It challenged me to take on a new role. To give up that role and take on another completely different. I realized that chasing money is less important than furthering my career and working with truly unique and incredibly talented individuals. I was hit with the realization that in order to be a good designer, I needed to continually push myself outside of my comfort zone and ask for help. I learned that code isn’t that scary, heck, I made this, as well as a bunch of other sites! All-in-all, 2013 was a delight and I hope that 2014 will continue down that path.
